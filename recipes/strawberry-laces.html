Strawberry Laces

Description

Strawberry cables are a confectionary product.

ASDA Strawberry cables are vegan, and contain no artificial flavours or colours.

Ingredients

  • 30 pence
  • Access to ASDA Online Groceries or a local store

Steps

  1. Visit ASDA's Online Groceries website.
  2. Add ASDA Strawberry Cables to your basket.
  3. Checkout and wait for the order to arrive.

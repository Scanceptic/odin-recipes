Paneer Korma

Description

Paneer korma is a creamy curry made with Indian cottage cheese, coconut milk and spices.

It is a classic vegetarian alternative to chicken korma, and it goes well with naan bread or flavoured rice.

Ingredients

For the Korma Sauce

  • 2tbsp oil
  • 1 green chilli cut into half
  • 1 inch ginger peeled, washed and roughly chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic peeled
  • 1 large onion thinly sliced
  • 1/4tsp turmeric
  • 1/2tsp chilli powder
  • 1/2tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2tsp garam masala

For the Paneer

  • 250g paneer
  • 1/2tbsp butter
  • 400ml coconut milk

Steps

  1. Heat the 2tbsp oil over a medium-high heat in a deep pan.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add ginger, garlic, green chilli and saute for a minute.
  3. Reduce the heat, add the onion and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix in turmeric, chilli powder, garam masala, coriander powder, and cumin powder, cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the paneer, coconut milk and butter into the pan, and simmer for 10 minutes.
